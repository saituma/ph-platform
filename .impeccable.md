## Design Context

### Users
- Primary users are both athletes and guardians using the mobile app.
- Context: on-the-go training guidance, video review feedback loops, and program navigation.
- Jobs to be done: follow training plans, upload feedback videos, and track progress with a coach.

### Brand Personality
- Premium, youthful, bold.
- Athletic and confident, with a modern, high-energy feel.

### Aesthetic Direction
- Bold/modern/athletic.
- Reference vibes: Instagram, Facebook, Telegram, X, and premium coaching apps.
- Avoid anything overly corporate or dull.

### Design Principles
1. Strong hierarchy and clarity at a glance (headline > key action > details).
2. Athletic energy with confident contrast, not flashy gimmicks.
3. Fast, tactile interactions with decisive motion and feedback.
4. Premium polish: consistent spacing, typography, and deliberate accent usage.
5. Designed for real-world use: readable, scannable, and resilient.
